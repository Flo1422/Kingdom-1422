Official Agreement

Non-Aggression Pact

The NAP keeps the peace between all alliances. Trust and order — not control.

No Attacking
No attacking cities, hitting gathering troops, or hitting alliance buildings at any time outside authorized PvP events.
No Poaching
Do not directly PM members of other NAP alliances to recruit them away.
No Banner Wrapping
Surrounding outposts with banners is a violation. The seeking alliance may remove one banner to make fair contact.
Inactive Players
Players offline 20+ days must be removed. Medical/personal exceptions handled by the King after each CB/KvK.
Statute of Limitations
All NAP trials must open within one week of the event. Independent resolutions between parties cancel the trial.
Max 4 Representatives
No more than 4 reps per alliance in NAP HOPE CHAT. Swappable freely — King enforces the limit.
Enforcement
1stFormal warning — screenshot required as evidence.
2ndBlacklist + kingdom-wide retaliation vote.
3rdPermanent isolation. Status: Free to Burn
