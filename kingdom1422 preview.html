Official Agreement

NAP – Non-Aggression Pact

The NAP exists to prevent internal war, protect player growth, and promote a strong united kingdom. Trust and order — not control.

🚫
Forbidden at All Times
No attacking cities · No hitting gathering troops · No hitting alliance buildings or banner zones. Applies to all NAP alliances without exception.
🐀
No Poaching
We do not directly PM members of other NAP alliances to recruit them away. Healthy competition, never manipulation.
⛳️
Banner Wrapping
Surrounding outposts with banners is a violation. If voted as such, the seeking alliance may remove one banner to make fair contact.
💀
Inactive Players
Players offline 20+ days should not be retained. King verifies activity after each CB/KvK — medical exceptions handled case by case.
⚖️
Statute of Limitations
All NAP trials must open within one week of the event. If parties resolve independently, the trial may be cancelled.
👥
NAP Representatives
Max 4 representatives per alliance in the NAP HOPE CHAT. Swappable freely. King enforces the limit.
⚠️ Enforcement
1stFormal warning — screenshot required as evidence.
2ndBlacklist + kingdom-wide retaliation vote.
3rdPermanent isolation. Status: Free to Burn.
